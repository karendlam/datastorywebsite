About Us

Welcome to DataStory, a premier student-run organization dedicated to providing top-notch consulting services in the fields of data science and software development. Our club is committed to helping businesses and organizations harness the power of data to make informed decisions and drive meaningful results. We also provide our members with multiple sources of professional development and opportunities, through workshops, case competitions, and speaker panels.

WHAT WE OFFER

Projects

Consultants are provided with an opportunity to work on semesterly projects of their choosing. We work with a variety of companies, ranging from startups to NGOS to well-establish corporations.

Community

We highly stress the importance of community within our club. We host many big social events, as well as events that focus on creating smaller bonds, in order to make sure long-lasting relationships develop!

Professional Development

Through our technical and behavioral teams, we provide a wide variety of professional workshops for our members to gain valuable soft and hard skills. Some past workshop topics include SQL, Acing the Behavioral Interview, and Breaking down the Product Management Process.
