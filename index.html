We Offer

Professional Development

Every semester, we hold workshops on interview preparation, resume advice, and internship tips. We understand the importance of real-world experience and strive to provide our members with the best practices.

Projects

All members will be able to contribute to a semesterly project of their choosing. We work with variety of companies from startups to well established corporations

Community

DataStory is a community of dedicated data scientists who are there to support eachother. All members are welcome to participate in monthly socials as well as DataStory Dates to get to know their club mates better and build long lasting relationships.

Get Involved

Students

We recruit new members every semester. We welcome all skill levels. Spring 2023 applications are open!

Companies

We actively look for companies to partner with every semester. Our data scientists are excited to work on any projects! If you have a project in mind, please contact us.
