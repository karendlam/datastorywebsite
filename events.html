EVENTS

how we continue to grow!

Professional Development Workshops

DataStory focuses on improving not only our technical skills, but our behavioral skills as well. Our Prof Dev teams focuses on creating interactive and in depth workshops targeting key soft and hard skills throughout the semester.

Case Competitions & Analytics Competitions

The best way to learn is to gain real life experience from multiple sources. We make sure to always have teams participating in premier case and analytics competitions throughout the country in order for our members to learn and compete with the best. DataStory is currently competing in the Ebay Machine Learning Competition.

Speaker Panels & Alumni Events

We pride ourselves in an extremely successful and interactive alumni. We make sure to host several speaker events throughout the semester, as well host our alumni mentorship program, in order to make sure our members are able to learn from those in the industry.
