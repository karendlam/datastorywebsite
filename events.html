Events

DataStory provides professional development workshops open to the public as well as data science bootcamps for members.

Professional Presentations

We hold three professional development presentations every semester covering a wide variety of topics including career fair strategies, insight on data science interviews, and networking tips. You do not need to be a member to attend these workshops. Look out on our google calendar for times!

Datascience workshops

All members have access to datascience bootcamps designed to teach members fundamentals in SQL, Python, R, and much more. Each semester, new members are taught concepts relevant to their project by existing members.
