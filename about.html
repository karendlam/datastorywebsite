WHO WE ARE

"community through data science"

OUR MISSION

DataStory
...was founded in order to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience necessary to tackle complex real-world challenges through the application of data science. We strive to foster a collaborative and inclusive environment that encourages innovation, professional growth, and societal impact, aiming to put our members on a successful path post-grad.

WHAT WE DO

DataStory is focused on creating value for startups, NGOs, and corporations in a various assortment of fields, including data analytics, software development, machine learning, marketing, and more. We are experienced in tackling a variety of data-related problems, as well as provide solutions to help shape a companies’ future.

NOT ONLY THAT,

Through our Exploration program, we help develop our members through educating them on not only data science technologies and techniques, but professional skills as well. We also host professional workshops that help our members grow hard and soft industry skills. Additionally, we host case competitions, speaker panels, and alumni events in order to provide our members with multiple opportunities to grow.
